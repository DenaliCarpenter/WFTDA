\hypertarget{website}{%
\subsection{Website}\label{website}}

Similarly to finding the URLs of the logo images, we want to find the
extensions for each of the leagues. We can find these in derbyTeams
stored in the ``a' objects. We then find the cases with''league\_names,"
and split the strings and unlist them until we have the exact extensions
we want. Then we simply paste the beginning of the URLs onto all of our
extensions.

\begin{Shaded}
\begin{Highlighting}[]
\NormalTok{derbyLink <-}\StringTok{ }\KeywordTok{html_nodes}\NormalTok{(derbyTeams, }\StringTok{"a"}\NormalTok{)}
\NormalTok{indexLink <-}\StringTok{ }\KeywordTok{str_detect}\NormalTok{(derbyLink, }\StringTok{"league_name"}\NormalTok{)}
\NormalTok{derbyLink <-}\StringTok{ }\NormalTok{derbyLink[indexLink]}
\NormalTok{derbyLink <-}\StringTok{ }\KeywordTok{str_split}\NormalTok{(derbyLink, }\StringTok{"><span"}\NormalTok{)}
\NormalTok{derbyLink <-}\StringTok{ }\KeywordTok{unlist}\NormalTok{(derbyLink)}
\NormalTok{derbyLink <-}\StringTok{ }\NormalTok{derbyLink[}\KeywordTok{grep}\NormalTok{(}\StringTok{"href"}\NormalTok{, derbyLink)]}
\NormalTok{derbyLink <-}\StringTok{ }\KeywordTok{str_split}\NormalTok{(derbyLink, }\StringTok{"="}\NormalTok{)}
\NormalTok{derbyLink <-}\StringTok{ }\KeywordTok{unlist}\NormalTok{(derbyLink)}
\NormalTok{derbyLink <-}\StringTok{ }\NormalTok{derbyLink[}\OperatorTok{-}\KeywordTok{grep}\NormalTok{(}\StringTok{"href"}\NormalTok{, derbyLink)]}

\NormalTok{derbyLink <-}\StringTok{ }\KeywordTok{paste}\NormalTok{(}\StringTok{"https://wftda.com"}\NormalTok{, derbyLink)}
